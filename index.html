Gun Violence Statistics

Here are some shocking statistics regarding gun violence:

  • There were over 43,000 gun-related deaths in the United States in 2020.
  • Approximately 1 in 3 Americans own a gun, but only 1 in 10 gun owners have been involved in gun-related violence.
  • Firearms are the leading cause of death for American children and teenagers.
